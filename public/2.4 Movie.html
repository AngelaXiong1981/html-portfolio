The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Dumb and Dumber

This is my favorite movie of all time. I love the silly characters and their journey on love and friendship.

The Shawshank Redemption

I love how the main character tries to stay hopeful throughout his 19 years in prison.

The Conjuring

The only horror movie that I like to rewatch when I'm in the mood for something scary.
